# FAQ

## How are savings estimates calculated?

Savings recommendations are generated using deterministic pricing and optimization rules rather than AI-generated calculations. This keeps estimates more explainable and predictable.

## Do I need to connect billing accounts?

No. BurnRateAI works using the plans, tools, and usage information you provide during onboarding.

## Are my company details public?

No. Public share links intentionally exclude identifying information such as email addresses, company names, and roles.

## Does this work for small teams?

Yes. The audit flow is designed for both small teams and larger organizations managing multiple AI subscriptions.

## Are the recommendations generated entirely by AI?

No. The optimization logic is rule-based for consistency. AI is primarily used for summarization only.
